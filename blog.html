Janardan Rai Nagar Rajasthan Vidyapeeth University is a Deemed University located in the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan. The University is registered under the Rajasthan Society Registration Act No. 28 that came into practice in 1958. JRNRVU was established in the year 1937 and since then offers various UG, PG and Diploma courses to the candidates. Janardan Rai Nagar Rajasthan University(JRNRVU) is located in Udaipur. It offers B.A, M.A, B.Com, M.Com, BSW, BBA, BBM, BCA, MBA, MCA and MSW at UG, PG, and Diploma levels. JRNRVU also offers PGDCA through distance learning. A 10th pass is required for Diploma programs while a 10+2 pass with relevant subjects is desired for UG courses. Janardan Rai Nagar Rajasthan Vidyapeeth University A Bachelor’s degree in the relevant discipline is mandatory for PG programs. Apart from the regular courses, the university also offers Lateral Entry admission to B.Tech and MCA. Admissions will be done on the basis of Entrance Test or Group Discussion or Personal Interview or Merit list in the qualifying examination followed by counselling. Preference will be given to State and National entrance scores at the time of admission to professional courses such as B.Tech, MBA and MCA. Janardan Rai Nagar Vidyapeeth University Application Form are available on the official website of University


Undergraduate Admission The University offers B.Tech, BBA, BCA / BCA (Lateral), B.A, B.Com, BSW, BAP and BCP program at the Undergraduate level. Admission to these courses will on the basis of marks acquired by the candidate in 10+2 in any discipline. Candidates applying for B.Tech course must have passed 10+2 with Physics Chemistry and Mathematics as main subjects. To apply for Diploma in Engineering a candidate must pass 10th with minimum required aggregate in Mathematics and Science. For B.Tech program selection is entirely based on the merit followed by the Counselling. Admission to Diploma, BCA / BCA (Lateral), B.A, B.Com, BSW, BAP, BCP courses will be done on the basis of marks secured in the Entrance test / Group Discussion / Personal Interview / Merit list.


Postgraduate Admission JRNRVU offers MBA, MCA / MCA (Lateral), PGDCA, M.Sc, M.A, M.Com and MSW courses at the Postgraduate level. Applying candidate must have obtained a Bachelor’s degree in the relevant discipline from a recognized University / Institute. Selection to certain PG courses is made on the basis of Entrance test / Group Discussion / Personal Interview / Merit list. Admission to MBA course will be on the basis of marks obtained by the candidate in graduation.


Janardan Rai Nagar Rajasthan Vidyapeeth University Facilities Library : The Institute has a well stocked and spacious library with more than 10,000 books. There are collection of books by Indian and Foreign authors on various functional areas of Paramedical Science, IT and Management. The University library has a collection of Dictionaries, Encyclopedia and Journals. Hostel : The Institute provides separate hostel facility for boys and girls. All hostels are facilitated with modern amenities like 24 hours security, mineral water, common – reading room, indoor games and telephone to ensure comfortable and homely stay for students.
